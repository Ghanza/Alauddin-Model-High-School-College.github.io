Our Vision






Under the blessings of Hazrat Pir Allaudin Siddiqui we believe that the life of our Prophet Hazrat Muhammad(P.B.U.H) is a role model for us to serve the nation and for creating the bright future of our students.

Today if there is any institution which exerts the greatest influence on children after the family, it is the school. The years that children spend in school are not merely time spent in learning and filling their minds with knowledge, but also time spent in moulding their character, acquiring various attitudes and imbibing basic principles of life.

The basic traits of their personality are formed during their school days. In our long-term vision for education we hope to help the students learn how to combat the negative feelings and stereotypes that plague our society. Rather, empowering them to see that they have the ability to be a great generation. Our responsibility in their lives is to ensure that our students are getting the best education we can provide tapping into the resources of our school, parents and the broader community so that they can succeed in whatever area they choose.


We believe school is a place of learning and a training ground for future citizens.
